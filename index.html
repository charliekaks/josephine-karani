Hi I'm Josephine Karani

I am a seasoned and career broadcast journalist for close to three decades.I have clear knowledge of media operations (print, electronic and internet). As Chairperson of the International Association of Women in Radio and Television (IAWRT-Kenya) I have demonstrated leadership in professional setups and networks that cuts across all spheres.

Broadcasting and TV Career

I have an extensive experience in TV and Broadcasting working for the National Broadcaster and The Parliamentary Service Commission. For my role in the national brodcaster Kenya Broadcasting Corparation(KBC) I left as Chief Producer (T.V) after rising through the ranks from an Assistant Producer, Producer III, Producer II, Producer I, Senior Producer, and Chief Producer (TV). In the Parliamentary Service Commission I was the Technical Manager, Broadcasting Unit, and head of the Parliamentary Broadcasting Unit (PBU), Parliament of Kenya.

Association and Media Groups

International Association of Women in Radio and Television (IWART)-Kenya chairman for nearly a decade. I'm currently the treasurer of the IWART International.
